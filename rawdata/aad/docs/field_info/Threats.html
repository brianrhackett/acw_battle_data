Detailed Field Information

File unit: Events File, 4/12/1861 - 5/13/1865
in the Series: Records About Civil War Battle Sites, created 1990 - 1993, documenting the period 4/12/1861 - 5/13/1865. - Record Group 220

Field Title: Threats (sample values)

Data Type : Alphanumeric
Start Position : 63
End Position : 66
Width : 4

Code List : View

Agency Notes:  The degree of anticipated threats to the battle sites' integrity over the next 10 years. The Commission assigned the degree of threat based on short- and long-term threats identified by the field investigator on the battle site survey form.


Sample Values:

High threats means that there is rapidly changing land use on or close to the battle site and large core parcels are threatened. Substantial loss of battle site landscape is expected within ten years.
Low threats means that land use on and close to the battle site are changing slowly and core parcels do not appear to be threatened. Minimal loss of battle site landscape is expected within ten years.
Moderate threats indicates that incremental changes in land use on or close to the battle site are occuring and smaller core parcels are threatened. Some loss of battle site landscape is expected within ten years.
Threat level is Not Applicable because the battle site landscape is deemed lost.
